Dice-Game
  • How to play
STEP 1: Click on the "Roll-dice" button to get a number, that will be added to your current score.
STEP 2: Click on the "Roll-dice" button again and again as many times you needed to raise your current score.
STEP 3: Click on the "Hold" button to save your current score to the Permanent Value and Pass on the turn to other Player whenever you think.
STEP 4: Remember! That if you got "1" as a value your current score will automatically reset to "0" and turn will be pass-on to other Player.
STEP 5: The One who reaches the target score first, will win the GAME.
